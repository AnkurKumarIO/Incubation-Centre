HOW IT WORKS

Mentor–Startup Matching Process

A structured, documented mentorship process designed to deliver real impact — not just introductions.

01

Onboarding & Profiling

On joining V3F, each startup completes a profiling session covering their sector, stage, key challenges, and mentorship needs.

02

Tailored Matching

V3F matches startups with mentors from the pool based on sector alignment, stage of development, and specific support requirements.

03

Logged Sessions

All mentoring sessions are logged and documented for record keeping and impact assessment. Both parties submit brief post-session feedback.

04

Annual Review

Mentors are onboarded for one year, with extensions based on performance and feedback from startups and V3F management.
