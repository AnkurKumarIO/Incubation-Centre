HOW IT WORKS

A structured mentorship journey.

A documented mentorship process designed to deliver real impact - not just introductions.

01person_search

Onboarding & Profiling

On joining V3F, each startup completes a profiling session covering their sector, stage, key challenges, and mentorship needs.

02hub

Tailored Matching

You’re sector-matched with mentors whose domain, network, and experience align best with your product and market.

03event_note

Logged Sessions

All sessions are logged and documented for record keeping and impact assessment. Both parties submit brief post-session feedback.

04reviews

Annual Review

Mentors are onboarded for one year, with extensions based on performance and feedback from startups and V3F management.

THE NETWORK

Domain experts for every startup.

V3F's mentor pool spans entrepreneurs, investors, corporate leaders, and domain specialists - each formally onboarded and sector-matched to your startup's exact needs. V3F matches startups with mentors from the pool based on sector alignment, stage of development, and specific support requirements.
