SELECTION CRITERIA

Startup Evaluation Matrix

Applications are scored against seven parameters. Strong teams with innovative ideas and a clear market opportunity score highest.

Innovation — Uniqueness of idea 20%
Team Capability — Skills & commitment 20%
Market Potential — Market size & demand 15%
Business Model — Revenue & scalability 15%
Technical Feasibility — Technology readiness 10%
Traction — Prototype, users, early validation 10%
Strategic Fit — Alignment with V3F focus areas 10%
info

Applications are reviewed by a committee comprising V3F management, VNIT faculty, industry experts, and investors.
