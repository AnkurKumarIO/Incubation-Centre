Technical & Business Readiness
Assess your current TRL and BRL
Technology Readiness Level (TRL) — Quick Guide
  • TRL 1 — Basic principles observed
  • TRL 2 — Technology concept formulated
  • TRL 3 — Proof of concept demonstrated
  • TRL 4 — Validated in lab
  • TRL 5 — Validated in simulated environment
  • TRL 6 — Prototype in simulated environment
  • TRL 7 — Prototype in real-world conditions
  • TRL 8 — Fully developed & tested
  • TRL 9 — Proven deployment
Business Readiness Level (BRL) — Quick Guide
  • BRL 1 — Market research done
  • BRL 2 — Product-market fit identified
  • BRL 3 — Team & business plan ready
  • BRL 4 — Customer segment validated
  • BRL 5 — Hypothesis testing underway
  • BRL 6 — MVP developed
  • BRL 7 — Customer feedback loop running
  • BRL 8 — Ready to scale
  • BRL 9 — Successfully scaled
