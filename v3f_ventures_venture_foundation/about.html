Mission

Building a Founder-First Ecosystem

V3F Nagpur provides startups with access to state-of-the-art facilities, a strong and well-connected network of mentors, valuable industry connections, and a supportive community that empowers entrepreneurs to grow, innovate, and thrive. Our objective is to create an environment that encourages sustainable entrepreneurship by providing the right ecosystem, support, and opportunities while contributing to long-term ecosystem development through continuous collaboration and innovation.

Startups incubated at V3F Nagpur also benefit from being a part of the broader VNIT innovation ecosystem. They gain access to the expertise and guidance of faculty members, well-equipped research infrastructure, talented student communities, meaningful industry partnerships, and established investor networks. Together, these resources provide startups with a strong foundation and a supportive environment that enables them to progress, collaborate, and grow throughout their entrepreneurial journey.

Governance

Governance Structure

V3F operates with clearly defined accountability structures to ensure transparency, fairness, and institutional alignment.

verified_user

Accountability Framework

  • The Director of VNIT Nagpur chairs the Board of Directors
  • Operational activities are reported to the Board quarterly
  • Policy revisions are made periodically by the Governing Board
  • Faculty startup and IPR matters are guided by VNIT policies
  • Disputes are resolved through mediation or arbitration
  • All startups submit audited financial statements annually
