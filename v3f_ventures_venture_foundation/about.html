Building a Founder-First Ecosystem

V3F Nagpur provides access to state-of-the-art facilities, a network of mentors, industry connections, and a supportive community that empowers startups to thrive. We aim to create an environment that encourages sustainable entrepreneurship and long-term ecosystem development.

Incubated startups gain access to the broader VNIT innovation ecosystem, including faculty expertise, research infrastructure, student talent, industry partnerships, and investor networks.

What Makes V3F Different

Six core principles that define our approach to building a founder-centric, sustainable incubation ecosystem.

🌱

Founder-Friendly Approach

Our incubation framework is designed to support entrepreneurs with the resources and guidance needed to build sustainable ventures, not just extract equity.

🏆

Competitive Landscape Awareness

Our policy is drafted considering the offerings of leading incubators across the region, ensuring startups receive best-in-class support.

♻️

Long-Term Ecosystem Development

V3F fosters long-term entrepreneurial growth and sustainable businesses that contribute positively to society and the Nagpur region.

🎓

Incentives for VNIT Faculty

Faculty engagement in mentoring startups is recognised by VNIT during appraisal and professional evaluation processes.

💡

Subsidised Services for VNIT Community

V3F provides subsidised access to incubation services for VNIT students and alumni, with significantly reduced fees compared to external applicants.

⚡

Operational Autonomy

V3F operates with functional autonomy to enable quick decision-making while remaining aligned with VNIT's broader institutional vision and policies.

Board of Directors
