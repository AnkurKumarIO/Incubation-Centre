What Makes V3F Different.

Six core principles that define our approach to building a founder-centric, sustainable incubation ecosystem.

01 APPROACH

Founder-Friendly Approach

Our incubation framework supports entrepreneurs with resources to build sustainable ventures — not just extract equity.

02 AWARENESS

Competitive Landscape Awareness

Our policy considers leading incubators across the region, ensuring startups receive best-in-class support.

03 ECOSYSTEM

Long-Term Ecosystem Development

V3F fosters entrepreneurial growth and sustainable businesses contributing positively to the Nagpur region.

04 FACULTY

Incentives for VNIT Faculty

Faculty mentoring startups is recognised by VNIT during appraisal and professional evaluation.

05 COMMUNITY

Subsidised Services for VNIT Community

VNIT students and alumni get significantly reduced fees compared to external applicants.

06 AUTONOMY

Operational Autonomy

V3F operates with functional autonomy for quick decision-making while aligned with VNIT's vision.

[ 04 ] GOVERNANCE

Governance Structure.

V3F operates with clearly defined accountability structures to ensure transparency, fairness, and institutional alignment.

verified_user

Accountability Framework

  • —The Director of VNIT Nagpur chairs the Board of Directors
  • —Operational activities are reported to the Board quarterly
  • —Policy revisions are made periodically by the Governing Board
  • —Faculty startup and IPR matters are guided by VNIT policies
  • —Disputes are resolved through mediation or arbitration
  • —All startups submit audited financial statements annually
policy

Key Policy Frameworks

how_to_vote

Selection Authority

Final incubation decisions rest with the V3F Governing Board or an authorised committee comprising faculty, industry experts, and investors.

policy

IP Ownership

IP created using VNIT resources is governed by the VNIT IPR Policy. Startups retain IP developed independently with V3F's guidance.

trending_up

Exit Strategy

V3F exits equity via founder buyback, secondary share sale, or acquisition. All exit terms are defined clearly at onboarding.
