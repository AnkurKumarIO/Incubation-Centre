ABOUT V3F NAGPUR

Championing the Entrepreneurial Ambition

True innovation begins where entrepreneurial ambition meets the right environment — and V3F Nagpur exists to create exactly that. As VNIT's dedicated incubation arm, we accelerate startups that generate meaningful impact, spark new ideas, and advance economic progress throughout the region.

MISSION & ECOSYSTEM

Crafting a Startup-Centric Environment

V3F Nagpur opens doors to world-class facilities, an experienced mentor community, strong industry linkages, and a collaborative atmosphere that gives startups every advantage to grow. Our core goal is to cultivate a space where responsible entrepreneurship and lasting ecosystem value go hand in hand.

Startups that join V3F tap into the full breadth of VNIT's innovation network — from academic experts and cutting-edge research labs to a pipeline of student talent, corporate collaborations, and active investor communities.

SALIENT FEATURES

The V3F Advantage

🌱

Built Around the Founder

Every element of our incubation model is structured to equip entrepreneurs with the tools, capital access, and advisory they need to build enduring companies — value creation takes precedence over equity extraction.

🏆

Benchmarked Against the Best

Our policies and support frameworks are informed by a thorough study of top-performing incubators across the region, so startups consistently receive industry-leading levels of assistance.

♻️

Sustained Regional Impact

V3F nurtures entrepreneurial journeys beyond the short term, helping build resilient, socially conscious businesses that leave a lasting positive mark on the Nagpur region and its communities.

🎓

Faculty Recognition & Rewards

VNIT formally acknowledges faculty contributions to startup mentorship as part of its academic appraisal and career progression evaluation framework.

💡

Preferred Access for VNIT Members

Current VNIT students and alumni enjoy preferential pricing on all incubation services — fees are set at a considerably lower rate than those charged to outside candidates.

⚡

Independent & Agile Operations

V3F maintains functional independence that allows swift, responsive decision-making — all while staying firmly anchored to the overarching strategic vision and guidelines set by VNIT.
